## TIPS FOR RECORDING

1. **Use 1920x1080 resolution** for clear screenshots
2. **Pause briefly** on each screen (2-3 seconds) for editing
3. **Use consistent mouse movements** — smooth and deliberate
4. **Pre-populate data** so charts and tables look meaningful
5. **Record audio separately** for better quality and editing flexibility

# Release v1.0.0 - Full Feature Release

**Date:** 2025-12-21

We are thrilled to announce **ArthSaarthi v1.0.0**! This is our first major stable release, representing a comprehensive personal portfolio management system. Building on feedback from our pilot versions, this release delivers a complete suite of features for tracking all types of investments, from stocks and mutual funds to fixed deposits and government savings schemes.

---

## âœ¨ What's New in This Release

### 1. Expanded Asset Class Support

Track your entire financial portfolio, not just stocks and mutual funds.

*   **Fixed Deposits (FDs):** Add and track bank FDs with principal, interest rate, and maturity date. View projected maturity value and XIRR.
*   **Recurring Deposits (RDs):** Track monthly RD installments with automatic maturity calculations.
*   **Public Provident Fund (PPF):** A specialized workflow with passbook-style view showing contributions, government interest credits, and current balance by financial year.
*   **Bonds:** Track corporate and government bonds with coupon rate, face value, and maturity details.

### 2. Corporate Actions & Dividend Tracking

Maintain accurate cost basis with proper handling of corporate events.

*   **Dividends:** Log cash dividends for stocks and mutual funds.
*   **DRIP (Dividend Reinvestment):** Record automatic reinvestment of dividends.
*   **Stock Splits & Bonus Issues:** Adjust your holdings when companies issue splits or bonuses.
*   **Tax Lot Selection:** Choose FIFO, LIFO, or specific lot when selling shares for accurate capital gains tracking.

### 3. ESPP/RSU Award Tracking

A dedicated workflow for employees with stock compensation.

*   **Award Entry:** Record vest date, quantity, and Fair Market Value (FMV) at vesting.
*   **Sell to Cover:** Track shares automatically sold to cover tax withholding.
*   **Cost Basis:** Automatic tracking of cost basis for tax purposes.

### 4. Foreign Stock & Currency Support

Invest globally without losing visibility.

*   **Multi-Currency Tracking:** Add assets in foreign currencies (e.g., USD, EUR).
*   **Automatic Conversion:** Portfolio values and P&L are automatically converted to your base currency (INR) using real-time and historical exchange rates.

### 5. Goal Planning & Watchlists

Plan for the future and monitor opportunities.

*   **Financial Goals:** Create goals (e.g., "Retirement", "House Down Payment"), link portfolios or assets, and track your progress.
*   **Watchlists:** Monitor assets you're interested in without adding them to your portfolio.

### 6. Transaction History Page

A dedicated page for viewing and managing all your transactions.

*   **Filterable List:** Filter by portfolio, asset, date range, and transaction type.
*   **Complete CRUD:** Edit or delete any transaction directly from the history view.

### 7. Enhanced Security & User Experience

*   **Audit Logging:** All sensitive operations (login, user management, data changes) are logged for security.
*   **User Profile Page:** Manage your account settings, change password, and backup/restore data.
*   **Inactivity Timeout:** Automatic session logout for security when you step away.
*   **Sectioned Holdings:** Holdings are now grouped by asset class (Stocks, Mutual Funds, Deposits, etc.) for easier navigation.

### 8. Improved Desktop Application

For users who prefer a standalone application without Docker.

*   **Multi-Platform Builds:** Dedicated installers for Windows, macOS (Apple Silicon & Intel), and Linux.
*   **Desktop-Optimized Admin:** Access Interest Rates and System Maintenance features (User Management hidden for single-user mode).
*   **In-App Help:** Context-sensitive help buttons (`?`) throughout the application link to the bundled User Guide.

## ðŸš€ How to Get Started

*   **For Desktop Users:** Go to the project's [**GitHub Releases**](https://github.com/aashishbhanawat/ArthSaarthi/releases) page and download the installer:
    *   **Windows:** `ArthSaarthi-Setup-1.0.0.exe`
    *   **macOS (Apple Silicon M1/M2/M3):** `ArthSaarthi-1.0.0-arm64.dmg`
    *   **macOS (Intel):** `ArthSaarthi-1.0.0.dmg`
    *   **Linux:** `ArthSaarthi-1.0.0.AppImage` or `.deb`

*   **For macOS Users:** After copying the app to Applications, run this command to allow it to open:
    ```bash
    xattr -cr /Applications/ArthSaarthi.app
    ```

*   **For Self-Hosters (Docker):** Follow the instructions in the [Installation Guide](https://aashishbhanawat.github.io/ArthSaarthi/installation_guide/) to deploy using Docker Compose.

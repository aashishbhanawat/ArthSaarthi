# ArthSaarthi: The Official User Guide (v1.2.0)

Welcome to ArthSaarthi, your premium companion for wealth management, multi-asset tracking, and automated taxation planning. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the platform's features and workflows.

---

## 1. Navigating the Platform

ArthSaarthi uses a streamlined, sidebar-driven navigation menu for effortless access to all financial modules.

### Sidebar Modules
- **Dashboard**: The central hub for your wealth metrics and top-level portfolio analytics.
- **Portfolios**: Manage distinct investment strategies (e.g., Core Wealth, Retirement, Alpha Trading).
- **Transactions**: A global, filterable ledger of every financial event across your account.
- **Goals**: Define and track progress for major milestones like "House Downpayment" or "Retirement".
- **Watchlists**: Monitor market prices and performance for assets you don't yet own.
- **Import**: Upload and process broker statements (CSV, XLS, PDF) with the intelligent Import Wizard.
- **Admin**: System-wide configuration for user management, interest rates, and symbol aliases.
- **Profile**: Manage your personal settings, default currency, and fiscal year start month.

### Adding Transactions
You can manually add a wide range of transaction types including:

- **Fixed Income**: PPF Contributions, FD Deposits, and RD Maturity tracking.

## 3. Advanced Asset Tracking

### Corporate Actions (Bonus & Splits)
ArthSaarthi automatically handles adjustments to your cost basis when corporate actions like Bonus Issues (e.g., RELIANCE 1:1) or Reverse Splits (e.g., HDFC BANK 1:2) occur.

![Asset Drill-down: Transaction History](file:///home/developer/pms/ArthSaarthi/temp_qa_run/media/reliance_transactions_1774597337140.png)

### Global Stocks & Multi-Currency
Track US-listed stocks (e.g., AAPL, MSFT) with automatic USD-to-INR conversion.
- Supports **Specific Lot Identification** to minimize taxes during a sale.
- Integrated historical FX rates for precise INR valuation.

![AAPL Transaction Logs (USD)](file:///home/developer/pms/ArthSaarthi/temp_qa_run/media/alpha_trading_aapl_transactions_1774585188225.png)
